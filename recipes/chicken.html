Chicken

Description

Juicy grilled chicken breasts marinated in herbs and spices, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. Pairs well with salad or rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Gralic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and spices in a bowl
  2. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  4. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side
  5. Let rest before serving
