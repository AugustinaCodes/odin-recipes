Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a comforting Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Perfect for a hearty family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles and set aside
  2. Brown ground beef and mix with marinara sauce
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, salt, and pepper
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella
  5. Repeat layers and top with parmesan
  6. Bake at 190C for 40 minutes
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving
