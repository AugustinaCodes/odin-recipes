Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and tangy salad made with crisp vegetables, feta cheese, and olives. A great side or light main dish.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces
  2. Combine in a large bowl with olives and crumbled feta
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice
  4. Sprinkle with oregano and toss gently
  5. Serve immediately or chill before serving
